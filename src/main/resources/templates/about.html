what is this?

AI tunnel is a small experiment in dynamic, AI-generated web components. Every page you visit is created on the fly.

There’s no caching here — so if you revisit a page, like /dogs or /rain, it will look different each time.

Each link brings you deeper down the tunnel, generating a new, unique page based on your current path.
